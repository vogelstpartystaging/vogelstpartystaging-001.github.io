2pm, 10th October 2015


The second Vogel Street Party is fast approaching. This year the party celebrates two themes.

LITERATURE

To celebrate Dunedin's creative city status as UNESCO City of Literature, alongside Edinburgh, Melbourne, Dublin, Prague & more.

LIGHT

As 2015 is the International Year of Light, the Vogel Street Party is Dunedin's major effort to join in the world-wide celebration of light & light-based technologies.

The events, exhibitions and activities this year will follow these themes and showcase the talent and creativity we have hidden in our humble city.
